Artist, character designer, irl chao

If its weird and sold 100 copies in 2002 I probably love it. Inspired by works like Chulip, MoonRPG, LSD: Dream Emulator, Digimon World, Spyro, and every single neopets petpage.

2013 I designed for TF2 and Dota2 in the Steam Workshop, creating official Valve merchandise with WeLoveFine. I also painted backgrounds for OK KO, Eddsworld, Homestuck, and Brawlhalla.

2016 onwards my focus has been concept art and character design. Most recently I spent over a year developing Boxed Up at Studio Thunderhorse, involved in fundamental decisions like "what if breaking rocks rarely reveals a lil bonus rat?"

Currently looking for long term work at game studios
