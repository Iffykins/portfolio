Ureleased Studio Thunderhorse project

Started as a 4 player rogue-like with party game elements!
Each character had their own themed map, weapons, and enemies:

(Fantasy, Tech, Candy, and Dreams)

As the project evolved we switched focus to a single player experience
following Chance as they process moving away from their friends.

Purely rogue-like with a charming cardboard diorama aesthetic.
